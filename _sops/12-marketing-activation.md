## Step-by-Step Checklist

### Step 1: Identify Your Channels
- [ ] List where your target users hang out online
- [ ] Prioritize 2-3 channels to focus on (not more)
- [ ] Create accounts / optimize profiles
- [ ] Start with channels you're already active on

**Channel Selection Matrix:**

| Channel | Best For | Effort | Timeline |
|---------|----------|--------|----------|
| Twitter/X | Devs, makers, B2B | Medium | Weeks |
| Reddit | Niche communities | Low | Days |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professionals | Medium | Weeks |
| Indie Hackers | Indie makers | Low | Days |
| Product Hunt | Launch spike | High | One day |
| SEO/Content | Long-term organic | High | Months |
| Cold email | B2B targeted | Medium | Days |

**Output:** 2-3 priority channels selected

### Step 2: Quick Wins (First 48 Hours)
- [ ] Share in your personal network
- [ ] Post in relevant online communities
- [ ] Reach out directly to people who need this
- [ ] Ask for shares from friends/supporters

**Personal Network Outreach:**
```markdown
## Quick Outreach List

### Friends & Family (Personal ask)
- [ ] [Person 1] - [Why they'd care]
- [ ] [Person 2] - [Why they'd care]
- [ ] [Person 3] - [Why they'd care]

### Professional Network (LinkedIn/Twitter DM)
- [ ] [Person 1] - [Connection to problem]
- [ ] [Person 2] - [Connection to problem]

### Online Communities (Post in)
- [ ] [Subreddit 1] - r/[subreddit]
- [ ] [Subreddit 2] - r/[subreddit]
- [ ] [Discord/Slack 1] - [community name]
```

**Direct Message Template:**
```
Hey [Name]!

I just launched [Product Name] - [one-line description].

I thought of you because [specific reason they'd benefit].

Would love your thoughts: [URL]

No pressure, but if you find it useful, a share would mean a lot!
```

**Output:** First 10-20 users from personal outreach

### Step 3: Reddit Strategy
- [ ] Find relevant subreddits (5-10)
- [ ] Understand each community's rules
- [ ] Provide value before promoting
- [ ] Share authentically when appropriate

**Reddit Posting Guidelines:**
```markdown
## Reddit Marketing Rules

### DO:
- Read subreddit rules first
- Contribute genuinely before promoting
- Share as "I built this" story, not ad
- Be honest about being the creator
- Engage with comments genuinely
- Share in r/SideProject, r/InternetIsBeautiful, r/indiehackers

### DON'T:
- Spam multiple subreddits same day
- Post and disappear
- Lie about being the creator
- Argue with negative feedback
- Post in irrelevant communities
```

**Reddit Post Template:**
```
Title: I built [Product Name] to solve [Problem]

After [struggling with X / talking to people who Y], I decided to build a solution.

[Product Name] lets you [main benefit].

Key features:
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

I'd love feedback on what could be better!

[URL]

(Yes, I built this - happy to answer any questions about the tech/journey)
```

**Output:** Posts in 3-5 relevant subreddits

### Step 6: SEO Basics (Long-term)
- [ ] Set up Google Search Console
- [ ] Create 3-5 landing pages for key terms
- [ ] Start a simple blog (optional)
- [ ] Get a few backlinks

**SEO Quick Wins:**
```markdown
## SEO Setup

### Google Search Console
1. Add your domain
2. Submit sitemap (usually /sitemap.xml)
3. Monitor for indexing issues

### Landing Page SEO
Each page should have:
- [ ] Unique title tag with keyword
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 with keyword
- [ ] Quality content (500+ words)
- [ ] Internal links

### Content Ideas
- "[Alternative] vs [Competitor] vs [Your Product]"
- "Best [category] tools in 2025"
- "How to [solve problem your product solves]"
```

**Output:** Basic SEO foundation in place

### Step 7: Cold Outreach (B2B)
- [ ] Build list of potential customers
- [ ] Personalize outreach messages
- [ ] Follow up appropriately
- [ ] Track response rates

**Cold Email Template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about [Their problem]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific observation about their company/role].

I built [Product Name] to help with [problem]. [One sentence about what it does].

Would you be open to a quick look? Here's a [demo video / free trial link].

No pressure either way!

[Your name]
```

**Follow-Up Template:**
```
Subject: Re: Quick question about [Their problem]

Hi [Name],

Just bumping this in case it got buried.

[Product Name] has helped [specific result or social proof].

Happy to answer any questions.

[Your name]
```

## Common Pitfalls & Fixes

| Pitfall | Why It Happens | Fix |
|---------|----------------|-----|
| Spreading too thin | Trying all channels | Pick 2-3, do them well |
| Pure promotion | Not adding value | Give before you ask |
| Giving up too fast | No immediate results | Commit to 30 days minimum |
| Ignoring analytics | "I'm posting, that's enough" | Track what works, double down |
| Spamming | Desperation | Quality > quantity, always |
